Symposium on Faculty Development for Future-Ready Teaching and Learning

Faculty across Asia face increasing demands to adapt teaching and mentoring practices to prepare students for the challenges of the 21st century. This symposium convenes international and regional leaders to share future-ready pedagogy, practical generative AI applications, and mentorship training.

Overview

The symposium highlights future readiness, high-impact teaching practices, generative AI integration, and faculty mentorship training, with opportunities for participants to showcase innovations through talks or posters.

Future Readiness

Rethink curriculum, employability, creativity, and learning for rapidly changing societal and workforce needs.

Practice-Focused AI

Hands-on applications of generative AI for teaching, learning design, and future skill development.

Mentorship & Engagement

Faculty mentorship training and practical approaches to engaging Gen-Z learners effectively.

Focus Areas

Core elements emphasized across keynotes, plenaries, and workshops.

High-Impact Teaching

Evidence-based practices that improve learning outcomes and student persistence.

Learning Design for Gen-Z

Designing effective pedagogies for engagement, motivation, and modern learning behaviors.

Regional Exchange

A platform for Asia-Pacific faculty networking, collaboration, and sharing of innovations.
